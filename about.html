About Pixelarticons

Pixelarticons was born from a passion for pixel art and the need for high-quality, deliberately pixelated icons that stand out in today's world of smooth, vector-based designs. Created with meticulous attention to detail, each icon is crafted to bring a touch of retro flair to modern digital interfaces.

Our icon library is designed primarily for unconventional, brutal, or minimalist websites where standard icon sets fall short. With over 2000 icons and counting, Pixelarticons offers a comprehensive collection that's perfect for designers looking to create unique user experiences that break away from the ordinary.

Every icon is optimized for scalability, performance, and visual consistency. Whether you're building a game interface, a creative portfolio, or an experimental web project, Pixelarticons provides the pixel-perfect assets you need to bring your vision to life.
